  • Goal: Create a continuous line from one side of the board to the other before the other player.
  • Player 1 uses solid lines and tries to connect the left and right sides (blue).
  • Player 2 uses dashed lines and tries to connect the top and bottom sides (red).
  • Players alternate placing their lines between the pegs on board. Solid goes first.
  • Players may not place a line on a peg that is in the opponent's border rows.
  • You may link to pegs which are your own. The links can only go between two pegs in a knight's move fashion and cannot cross another link of the opponent's but can cross your own.
  • You win if you make a continuous chain of linked pegs connecting your border rows. If neither side can achieve this, the game is a draw.

Public. Anyone can join. First come first serve.

Private. You must send game link to a player.
